about us
Isolating Together is a movement of everyday people who have grown tired of the cynicism, frustration, and loneliness overwhelming our society. We are coming together to build a one-stop-shop for hope and support–a place that channels all this negative energy into working to make things better. How?
  • Acting Together: By taking on approachable action items, we will uplift ourselves and our neighbors through collective volunteerism and mass demands for better.
  • Creating Together: By providing an outlet for people to share their creative work, we can replace the negative emotions holding us back with a spirit of comfort and love, all while supporting our artists and creators.
  • Community Building: By being a part of this community, we are being hands-on in constructive and creative ways. We are breaking down any artificial barriers that make us feel so hopeless and alone–we are uniting locally, regionally, and nationally in a positive movement, even while we might be physically apart.
We are a group of college students from all across the country who are taking on the deep cynicism, frustration, and pain ravaging our society. There have been long-standing, structural issues in our nation that have led to our unequal, atomized response to this virus, which reflects who exactly is disproportionately impacted by the virus. We know that a large majority of people know we haven’t faced this crisis properly, and know that some people are being hurt by this way more than others are. We are creating a national, people-powered (not-for-profit!) movement to rally this mass of people who know we can do better. It’s easy to feel frustrated about things. It’s less easy to do something about them, especially when there’s no clear mission bringing us together. We’re here to provide it.
We’re building something that brings us together, fosters hope and support, and gives us real, actionable things we can do to make things better. Our broad goal is to bring as many people to our table as possible, particularly so we can rally around the creation of something like the Pandemic Testing Board–a program to bring about massive testing and contact tracing, all while creating thousands upon thousands of jobs. We want to lobby for this sort-of mass action as the only appropriate response to such a national crisis, all while using our voices to hold any mass action to account. We want to ensure none of these projects discriminate against, or endanger, any populations, especially given the knowledge that certain populations are already facing heightened threats to this crisis.
To help make this vision a reality, please be sure to take part in the components of our site: volunteer through Acting Together (link), uplift us through Creating Together (link), and inspire others through Inspiring Together (link). And importantly, we need help spreading the word. Keep up to date with our movement on Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram. Share our movement with those who trust you most: friends, family, and neighbors. Our ambitions are high, but the moment demands it. Together, we can create a real movement of hope, love, and support. We can do this.
