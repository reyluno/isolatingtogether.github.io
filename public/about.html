ABOUT US

We are an anti-racist, not-for-profit organization fighting for love-based action in the face of the COVID-19 crisis. There are long-standing, structural issues in our nation that have led to our unequal, atomized response to this virus, issues reflected in the specific populations Coronavirus disproportionately impacts. We know we can do more to address this crisis, and to help those who are disproportionately hurt by it, so we have created a platform to do just that.

Our broad goal is to rally as many people as possible around the creation of a Pandemic Testing Board–a program to bring about massive testing and contact tracing while creating thousands of jobs. We demand that the needs and voices of marginalized and disproportionately impacted populations are centered in any national project–we look to the Movement for Black Lives National Demands for COVID-19 as a framework for lawmakers to align with. Further, we aim to connect people to existing organizations and volunteer efforts that have long been doing the work to combat racist systems and fight for equitable outcomes.

To help make this vision a reality, please be sure to take part by:

  • getting directly involved on our team
  • volunteering through Acting Together
  • uplifting us through Creating Together
