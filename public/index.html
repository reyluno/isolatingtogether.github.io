Our Mission

Isolating Together is a movement against cynicism, frustration, and loneliness. We built a hub for hope and support–a place that channels negative energy into positive action by:
ACTION ITEM NO. 1
Demand Mass National Testing and Contact Tracing
We must face this moment with the massive response it demands. We need the Pandemic Testing Board–a coordinated effort to create hundreds of thousands of jobs in order to produce tests, test every single person in this country, and contact trace every person who tests positive.
ACTION ITEM NO. 2
Donate to Covid Protest Relief Fund
SNL’s Chris Redd has created a fund to assist those who contract COVID-19, or sustain other injuries, while protesting; the duns will provide support for treatment/hospital bills, bail relief, and court expenses for protestors.
ACTION ITEM NO. 3
Check In
Check in with a Neighbor, Friend, or Family Member You Haven’t Talked To In A While. Our mission is one of care and love. We are all facing different levels of financial woes, threats to our health, and frustration with our brutal systems. That’s why it’s important that we meet this deeply challenging moment head-on and show those around us how much we care about them, even if we haven’t spoken in a while.
