Nourish NYC

NourishNYC is a non-profit organization dedicated to keeping New York City-based activists safe, healthy and free. They partner with local businesses to provide monetary and material support for demonstrators, including bail funding, first aid, PPE, food, water, and transportation.

COOPERATION JACKSON

Cooperation Jackson is a Jackson, Mississippi-based cooperative network with the vision of uplifting underserved communities by providing an alternative to capitalism. Focused primarily on empowering Black and Latinx communities, the goal of the cooperative is to establish four intrinsically connected and dependent organizations: “a federation of local worker cooperatives, a cooperative incubator, a cooperative education and training center (the Kuwasi Balagoon Center for Economic Democracy and Development), and a cooperative bank or financial institution.” Cooperation Jackson envisions a democratized economy and society, established by and for the workers and built on equity and sustainability.

PEOPLE WANT CHANGE

This twitter thread by journalist Anne Helen Peterson shows photos from dozens of protests across the country, from cities with populations in the hundreds of thousands to towns with populations under 1,000. The powerful imagery and wide-sweeping nature of these protests are important to remember given the media’s all-too-common focus on major cities. Peterson’s thread hammers home the message that this is truly a national, even global movement, one primarily rooted in peace and a dominant need for systemic change.
